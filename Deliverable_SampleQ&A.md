Below are example interactions that reflect how the assistant responds. Answers are summarized; actual responses may have minor wording differences but will keep the same facts and source links.

### 1. Expense ratio – Large Cap

- **User question**  
  `What is the expense ratio of Nippon India Large Cap Fund Direct Growth?`

- **Assistant answer (example)**  
  **Answer**: The expense ratio for **Nippon India Large Cap Fund** is **0.67% for Direct plan** and **1.49% for Regular plan**.  
  **Source**: `https://mf.nipponindiaim.com/InvestorServices/FactsheetsDocuments/Fundamentals-November-2025/Innerpage/Large-Cap.html`

---

### 2. Exit load – Flexi Cap

- **User question**  
  `What is the exit load for Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund Direct Growth?`

- **Assistant answer (example)**  
  **Answer**: For **Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund**, up to 10% of the units allotted may be redeemed without any exit load on or before completion of 12 months from the date of allotment. Any redemption in excess of this in the first 12 months is subject to **1% exit load**; **Nil thereafter**.  
  **Source**: `https://mf.nipponindiaim.com/InvestorServices/FactsheetsDocuments/Fundamentals-November-2025/Innerpage/Flexi-Cap.html`
